## 2. Blue Jay Upgrade

**Blue Jay (`bird_blue-jay`)** reclassified from `seed_disperser` to `ecosystem_engineer`.

- Updated `keystone_type`: `"ecosystem_engineer"`
- New `keystone_description`: cites Pesendorfer et al. 2016 / Cornell Lab research on long-distance acorn dispersal in fragmented landscapes (3,000–5,000 acorns/fall, up to 1 mile per cache)
- Updated `functional_description`: emphasizes role in connecting isolated forest patches

**3 new symbiosis entries:**
- Blue Jay ↔ White Oak (mutualism, critical)
- Blue Jay ↔ Northern Red Oak (mutualism, critical)
- Blue Jay ↔ group_oaks (mutualism, critical) — genus-level long-distance dispersal
